## Deployment Tiers

### Tier 1 — Evaluation / Sandbox
- Non-production use only
- Full API access with test data
- 30-day evaluation period
- Contact for access

### Tier 2 — County Government
- Single county health department deployment
- Up to 500,000 members
- Full system including desktop + web portal
- 1-year support and maintenance included
- Training for up to 20 staff

### Tier 3 — National Government / SHA
- Unlimited members
- Full deployment with integration support
- Dedicated support team
- Custom SLA
- On-site implementation assistance

### Tier 4 — Private Health Insurer
- Up to 1,000,000 members
- White-label option available
- Custom branding
- API access included
- Priority support

### Tier 5 — API Access Only
- For hospitals, clinics, and third-party apps
- Per-API or bundle pricing
- Monthly flat fee or per-call billing
- Email support included

### Tier 6 — SaaS (Cloud-Hosted)
- For NGOs, county hospitals, SACCOs
- Up to 50,000 members
- Hosted, maintained, and updated automatically
- No infrastructure management required
